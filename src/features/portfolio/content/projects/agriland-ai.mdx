## Introduction

Built during a 24-hour hackathon with a small team: an AI platform that helps farmers and investors find the best land for a given crop, by analyzing soil, climate, topography, and water availability instead of relying on guesswork or a local expert's memory.

---

## 🌟 Key Features

- **Interactive Land Map** – search and visualize parcels using Leaflet and Google Maps.
- **AI Suitability Scoring** – get High/Medium/Low suitability scores for crops like wheat, rice, maize, and vegetables.
- **Multi-Factor Analysis** – soil pH, NPK levels, texture, climate, topography, and water availability, all factored in.
- **Crop Recommendation Engine** – suggests the best crops for a given parcel automatically.
- **Exportable Reports** – download suitability results as PDF or CSV.

---

## 🛠 Tech Stack

- **Framework:** Next.js 14 (App Router), TypeScript
- **UI:** Tailwind CSS, shadcn/ui
- **Maps:** Leaflet, Google Maps
